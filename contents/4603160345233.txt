Details offers the feature to set up contract templates, especially for complex contracts this will be time saving.
Go to LABEL / ROYALTY and set up a royalty account Contract Templates, open subtab Contracts and click the green PLUS icon to add a new contract.
Enter your royalty rates, recoupable settings, etc.
To make the contract available as template, go to the subtab SPECIALS to check off the Template checkbox and add your template name.
All your template contracts will be available in the New Contract modal.
Read here how to copy an existing contract.

To add custom fields to a promoters page, go to SETTINGS / BOOKING and then click the second subtab REQUEST SETTINGSOn this tab, you will have the option to add custom fields for each of the different categories in details including the promoter. Once you've created your custom fields, go on to the PROMOTER section within BOOKING. Select your Promoter and go to tab ADDITIONAL INFO to find and populate the fields you've created.

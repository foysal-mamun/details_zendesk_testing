In General,Google cal has no auto synchronization feature.But you can do it by a little change in iCal link:To auto synchronize your iCal Feed with your Google calendar change the "webcal" part to "https".Example:Link of iCal Feed: "webcal://ical.detailsdetails.eu/artist/..."Link to be Pasted in Google Calendar:"https://ical.detailsdetails.eu/artist/..."This will enable your iCal Feed to auto sync with Google calendar.

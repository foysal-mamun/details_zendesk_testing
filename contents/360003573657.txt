On PRODUCTS / ROYALTYyou find an overview of all ROYALTY ACCOUNTS which are involved in the product.The list of royalty accounts includes the royalty account name and the related contract name. You can edit the royalty accounts and royalty contracts by clicking on the BOLD royalty account name or royalty contract name in the first and second columns of the list.The list also shows you a summarized contract info on royalty rates for physical, digital and licencing income. To activate RECOUPABLES for a single royalty account you tick the RECOUPABLES box in the fifth column.You can exclude single royalty accounts for that product by ticking the EXCLUDE box in the sixth column.

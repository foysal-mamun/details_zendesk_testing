To analyse your repertoire and sales, details offers extensive sales and stock reporting on Distribution / Reports.On the first subtab Reports all available sales reports can be filtered & exported to excel.Available reports are:- Stock Report- Label Report- Release Exports- Product Exports- Open Order Report- Summarized Reports- Raw sales reportsOn the subtab Release Report you can generate an easy and nice-looking profit & loss PDF report by release.
If you wish to customize this onesheet release report, please get in touch.

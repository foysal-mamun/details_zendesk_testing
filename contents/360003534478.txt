If you are experiencing problems with your Details account, like calendars not downloading or the computer stuck in old patterns, you might need to refresh your cache memory.
Here is how to do it If you have a MAC:
1. Go to Firefox -> Preferences
2. Then: Advanced -> Network  If you want more info about the cache, please read this article.

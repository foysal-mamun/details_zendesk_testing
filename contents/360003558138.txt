To activate BATCH STATEMENTS go to LABEL / PLUGIN and click BATCH STATEMENTS PROCESSING.1) Now go to LABEL / ROYALTY and select the subtab ALL ROYALTY STATEMENTS. At the bottom of the list you can now find and click the button BATCH STATEMENTS.::::2) Type in the STATEMENT YEAR and PERIOD & filter by Invoice Project and Royalty Period. Select manually or click Select Pending. Click [OK] to proceed.::::All statements will now be calculated, you will be notified by e-mail when the process is complete.Read more on royalty statements here.

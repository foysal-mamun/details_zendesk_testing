Official company invoice project
Internal invoice project (mainly for your sales imports that are usually self-billing and you don't need an additional invoice)
Royalty invoice project with royalty statement layout and related royalty credit notes if applicable.
The invoice projects for companies are most relevant for your invoice layouts and automatic numbering.
All invoice projects are customizable in Settings / Identities. You can add more invoice projects and change layout, leading texts, tax settings, etc for existing ones.

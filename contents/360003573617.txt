On PRODUCTS / SPECIALS you can assign territory rights to your product.By clicking the green PLUS on top right of the COUNTRY RESTRCITIONS box you can add countries which you do not have the rights for. You can add, edit more territories and territory groups on the SETTINGS / GENERAL SETTINGS.At the bottom you can invert the set up country restrictions to allowed countries by clicking INVERT COUNTRIES.Clicking REMOVE COUNTRIES will remove ALL country information that are set up.

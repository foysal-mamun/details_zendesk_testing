You can export accounting reports including withholding tax on Accounting / Plugins.1) First, activate the Plugin Invoice List with Exrates2) Now you can export the report by clicking the green sign right next to the Plugin name.3) If you want you can filter by Invoice Projects, Payment Status, Source and select the report format HTML, XLS or JSON4) Click submit to get your report.

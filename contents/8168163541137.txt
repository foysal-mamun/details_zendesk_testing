On RELEASES / OTHER INCOME you can manually add incomes that are not related to a specific product. Typical release incomes are sponsoring or fundings.
To add new income, click the green PLUS on top right of the list, enter the income date and a description.Select an INCOME GROUP - which can be set up under SETTINGS.Enter the amount and save.
To recoup any of your incomes, click ACCOUNT RECOUPABLES.

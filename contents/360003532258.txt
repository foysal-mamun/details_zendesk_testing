Follow these Steps to add Details Calendars to Google Calendar:1. Go the the 'Artist profile' - Web ICal Links section
2. Take the steps to create a Link - choose the info you want to be seen in that link
3. When the link is created, you have to pick the link you receive in the email (### iCal Feed ###).
4. You copy paste this link to your Google Calendar
5. Add Calendar, without making it public

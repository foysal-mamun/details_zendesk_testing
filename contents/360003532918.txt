From a Google Calendar1. Go to the calendar you want to share.
2. Click on 'Share this Calendar' (there is no need to tick the 'Public this Calendar' section)
3. Then choose 'Calendar Details' tab
4. Go to: Private Address and choose the 'ICal link'
5. Paste this exact link on the Artist defaults -  ICal web links - External dates section.

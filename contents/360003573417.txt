ERROR MESSAGE "Message from DHTMLSuite.dynamicContent The ajax request for distribution.pl failed"This message appears, when the internet connection between your computer and the server has been temporarily interrupted while trying to load data.In Most cases a simple REFRESH of the FIREFOX browser window will be enough to solve the issue (CMD + R on Mac or CTRL + R on PC). A Refresh will reload the requested browser page.

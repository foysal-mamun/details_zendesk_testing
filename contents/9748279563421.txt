There are several ways to include your contacts in Details, the most popular being
Import contacts from a XLSX file
Import contacts from a VCard or GroupVCard (for mac users)
Batch import of your contact database directly into details database
Add your contacts manually
You can find instructions for the individual processes in the linked articles.

If you wonder why you can't find the distributor on IMPORTS / OVERVIEW after creating the distribution account, you might have missed the last step of adding an import setup.
For each sales and/or cost statement, you will need a so-called statement setup which then creates a tile on your Imports / Overview grid. The statement setup includes mandatory info such as account name, currency and channel of statements to expect.
Read HERE how to add an Import Setup.
